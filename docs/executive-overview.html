GCP Role Lookup Tool

A lightweight IAM analysis tool that turns raw GCP reference data into faster, safer least-privilege decisions.

The Problem

IAM Reviews Are Slow and Error-Prone

Google's IAM reference is a lookup tool, not a decision tool. Operational workflows require extra manual steps that increase risk and slow teams down.

🔍

Manual & Lookup-Based

The public IAM reference does not support operational workflows out of the box.

  • Role titles must be resolved to IDs manually
  • No side-by-side role comparison
  • No way to search by permission
⚠️

Over-Provisioning Risk

Without easy overlap detection, teams grant more access than required.

  • Redundant roles go undetected
  • Least-privilege is hard to verify
  • Audit findings and policy exceptions increase
⏱️

High Research Overhead

Every IAM request requires extra manual research and back-and-forth.

  • 15–30 min lost per IAM request
  • Inconsistent decisions across teams
  • Extra effort converting to Terraform configs
The Solution

GCP Role Lookup Tool

A self-service Streamlit web app that replaces manual IAM research with structured, automated analysis. It maps role titles, permissions, and coverage in one place — and outputs results in Terraform-ready format.

  • Resolve human-entered role titles to canonical GCP role IDs
  • Compare permission coverage across multiple roles side by side
  • Detect redundant or overlapping role grants automatically
  • Find the narrowest role that satisfies a required permission set
  • Search any GCP permission to find which roles grant it
  • Export results directly as Terraform-ready IAM configuration
